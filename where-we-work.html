Where We Work

The global movement for safe, legal abortion is growing. Our staff and partners in countries as diverse as Bolivia, Zambia and India are working to ensure women and girls can access safe abortion care. Ipas has offices on four continents and in countries with high rates of death and injury from unsafe abortion.
