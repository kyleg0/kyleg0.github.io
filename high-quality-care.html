Ensuring high-quality care

Making high-quality abortion care a priority—even in the world’s most remote regions

SHARE  

No matter where they live, women and girls should have access to the highest possible quality of abortion and contraceptive care. To ensure high-quality safe abortion care, we train doctors, midwives and nurses to safely and respectfully perform abortions and provide counseling on contraceptive options health professionals. We also equip health facilities, provide clinical guidance and help keep clinics stocked with necessary supplies.

These are Ipas’s areas of expertise, and we draw on decades of experience helping countries improve their health systems’ ability to provide safe abortion care.

After enrolling in an Ipas-supported clinical training program, Ghana midwife Helena Adegi helped to bring safe abortion care to the Hobor community health center.
