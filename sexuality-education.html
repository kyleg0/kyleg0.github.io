Sexuality Education that includes abortion

Comprehensive sexuality education programs are proven to have a positive impact on young people’s sexual and reproductive health—and their ability to make safe and informed decisions. But the topic of abortion remains absent from most governmental and non-governmental programs, even in places where abortion is legal. This diminishes young people’s ability to avoid the dangers of unsafe abortion, to make fully informed choices, and to exercise their right to safe, legal abortion.

Ipas partners with global, national and local institutions to ensure non-biased information about abortion is included in comprehensive sexuality education programs. Drawing on our experience working with diverse stakeholders to promote safe abortion care, we work to hold governments accountable to their obligation to provide comprehensive information on sexual and reproductive health to all young people.
