Privacy, Content and Copyright Policies

Ipas respects privacy and is committed to protecting the privacy of users of our websites, applications and other online services. This policy applies to all information received by Ipas both online and offline, as well as any electronic, written, or oral communications. If you have any questions about Ipas or this privacy policy, please contact us.

Information We Collect

The information we receive and collect differs depending upon what you do when you visit our website. We collect information when you:

  • Make a donation
  • Sign an online petition
  • Apply for a job with Ipas
  • Contact us with questions, comments or requests
  • Sign up to receive updates and/or email newsletters from Ipas

Additionally, we and our service providers may automatically collect certain technical information from your computer or mobile device over time and across our digital presences using cookies or similar technologies. This information allows us and our service providers to recognize you and personalize your experience if you return, to improve our web and email services, and to provide you with content targeted to your interests.

How we use Collected Information

Ipas may use your personal information:

  • To provide you with information you requested
  • To process any transactions you authorized
  • To contact you about fundraising campaigns and/or advocacy efforts
  • To process an employment application
  • To personalize your interaction with Ipas such as customized web and email content based on your interests, information about events in your area and otherwise tailor content and communications for you
  • For our internal purposes such as:
    • Detect, prevent and respond to fraud, intellectual property infringement, violation of our terms of use or law, or other misuse of online services
    • Data analysis and audits to help us enhance and improve our online services, including usage trends, determining the effectiveness of our campaigns and operating and expanding our work.

Your personal information may be disclosed in any event as required by law; including: applicable laws in or out of your country of residence, to comply with legal processes, and/or to respond to requests from legal or government authorities.

How Can You Stop Receiving Communications from Ipas?

Ipas adheres to a strict opt-in policy regarding supporter information. We only place your information on a list at your request, and we will immediately take your information off of any list at your request. To opt out of receiving email from Ipas:

  • Use the links in the bottom of marketing emails Ipas sends to you to opt-out; or
  • Contact us – please provide your name and the email address you used to subscribe.

Third Party Services

Ipas engages the services of trust 3rd party services to provide additional functionality and services on our website; including but not limited to: Mailchimp, Authorize.net, ShareThis and Google Analytics. We do not partner or have special relationships with any ad server companies.

Donating to Ipas

Ipas is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. All contributions to Ipas are tax deductible to the full extent allowed by law. Ipas will not trade, share or sell a donor's personal information with anyone else, nor send donor mailings on behalf of other organizations. All information concerning donors or prospective donors is kept strictly confidential by Ipas and its staff unless permission is obtained from the donor to release such information, or unless disclosure is required by due legal process.

For online donations, Ipas has entered into a contract with Authorize.net to provide a secure online connection to allow you to transmit credit card information for your online donation. Authorize.net will be responsible for the security of the financial information (such as credit card information) that you provide. Please review Authorize.net's privacy policy for additional information concerning the privacy of your information and the security measures Authorize.net employs.

After you make your online donation, Authorize.net then provides information about you and your donation to us. The only information we receive is your name, address, telephone number, e-mail address, the amount of your donation, and any additional message you submitted along with the donation. We use this information to acknowledge your gift, and to keep records regarding our donors for administrative and tax purposes.

Ipas recognizes that the operation of the organization requires the maintenance and management of extensive donor and prospect records. Donor records often contain sensitive information that has been shared with or developed by Ipas staff on a confidential basis. "Records" means all files, including electronic data, containing information on donors or prospective donors to Ipas. The use of donor information is limited to internal purposes, by authorized individuals, to better service existing donors, potential donors or partner relationships. We make every effort to ensure the security of this information. To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the proper use of information, we have established and implemented appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect.

We may publish lists of Ipas's donors in various donor publications, but not online. We may include the names of donors unless you indicate that you prefer to remain anonymous.

If you indicate that your company has a matching gift program and provide the required form, we will use that information to process the matching gift from your company, to update our donor list, to acknowledge the company, and to include the company's name on published lists of Ipas's donors (unless we are notified that the company would prefer to remain anonymous). We will not initiate any further contact with the company unless you or the company requests that we do so.
