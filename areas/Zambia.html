Zambia

Although abortion is legal in Zambia, the barriers to accessing safe abortion care are formidable. Millions live on less than $1 a day, doctors are in short supply, and there is a strong and persistent social stigma surrounding abortion.

Ipas has worked in Zambia since 2006, focusing on the broad goal of reducing abortion-related deaths and injuries. This includes working with the Ministry of Health and other partners to expand access to safe and comprehensive abortion services, train providers and pharmacists, equip health-care facilities, and mobilize youth and community partners to raise awareness about reproductive health and rights.
